About Me

Hi, I'm Jenna. I'm a junior front-end web developer with the crazy, chaotic, fun role of mum to 4 cute cherubs.

In an earlier part of my life, I was a rural sole Podiatrist and Clinical Educator with a post-graduate certificate in Wound Care, working within the High Risk Feet area in hospital and community settings.

In 2022, I took the leap to change the course of my career, and was selected to participate in the SheCodes Plus course in Brisbane. Technologies I have mastered through this course include HTML, CSS, Python, Django, DRF, JavaScript, and ReactJS.
